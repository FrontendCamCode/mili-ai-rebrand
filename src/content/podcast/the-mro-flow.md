This foundational episode maps the complete flow of indirect materials through an asset-intensive operation — from the moment a maintenance need is identified to the moment the job is completed and unused materials are returned to stores.

Understanding the full flow is the starting point for identifying where breakdowns occur and where improvement effort should be focused.

**Key topics:**
- The eight steps in the MRO material flow
- Where the most common failure points occur
- How each step creates (or destroys) value for the operation
- The connection between flow discipline and maintenance schedule compliance
