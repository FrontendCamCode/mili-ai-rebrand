ISO14224 provides a standardised taxonomy for failure modes and mechanisms in oil, gas, and petrochemical industries — and increasingly beyond. The connection between structured failure coding and the material master is underexplored: the materials involved in a failure event, and the quality of their records, directly affects the value of the failure data collected.

This episode explores the practical intersection of ISO14224 failure coding, material data quality, and reliability improvement programmes.

**Key topics:**
- What ISO14224 covers and why it matters beyond oil and gas
- How failure codes connect to material records
- The data quality requirements for meaningful failure analysis
- Practical implementation guidance for organisations starting from scratch
