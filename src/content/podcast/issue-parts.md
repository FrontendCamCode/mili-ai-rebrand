Issuing parts is the moment where MRO inventory meets the maintenance work order. Done well, it's seamless. Done poorly, it creates inaccurate inventory records, job delays, and a stores team drowning in manual corrections.

This episode covers the process and system controls that make parts issuing accurate and efficient — from kitting for planned work to emergency counter issues for breakdowns.

**Key topics:**
- Planned vs. unplanned issues and how to handle both
- The importance of work order linkage at issue
- Over-issuing and how it inflates consumption data
- Kitting as a best practice for planned maintenance
