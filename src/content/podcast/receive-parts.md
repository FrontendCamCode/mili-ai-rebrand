Goods receipt is the first transaction in the inventory lifecycle — and the one that sets the tone for everything that follows. If materials are received inaccurately, against the wrong purchase order, or into the wrong location, the inventory record is wrong from day one.

This episode covers the process disciplines and system controls that make receiving accurate and efficient — and the common errors that create downstream problems.

**Key topics:**
- Three-way matching and why it matters
- Physical storage at receipt vs. system location
- How receipt errors compound into planning problems
- The receiving inspection question for critical materials
