Mark Cooper has led procurement transformation programmes across some of Australia's largest mining and utilities operations. He joins Tim and Justin for a candid conversation about what it actually takes to change the way a large organisation buys and manages its indirect materials.

**Conversation highlights:**
- What separates successful procurement transformation from failed ones
- The change management dimension that technical programmes underestimate
- How to build a business case for MRO investment that gets executive support
- Mark's perspective on where technology adds genuine value vs. where it creates complexity
