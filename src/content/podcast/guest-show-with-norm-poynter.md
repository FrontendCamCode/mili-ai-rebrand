Norm Poynter brings decades of experience in asset management and maintenance strategy across heavy industry. In this guest episode, Tim and Justin invite Norm to share his perspective on the relationship between maintenance strategy and MRO supply chain performance — a connection that's often discussed but rarely explored in depth.

**Conversation highlights:**
- How maintenance strategy shapes material demand
- The feedback loop between maintenance execution and supply chain design
- Where MRO investment decisions are made well — and where they're not
- Norm's view on where the discipline needs to go over the next decade
