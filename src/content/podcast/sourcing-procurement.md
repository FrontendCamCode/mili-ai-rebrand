Most MRO sourcing decisions are made reactively — a purchase order is needed, a supplier is selected, an order is placed. The strategic dimension of MRO sourcing — supplier rationalisation, panel management, contract coverage versus spot buying — is often underdeveloped relative to direct procurement.

Tim and Justin discuss the sourcing decisions that have the biggest impact on MRO performance and cost, and how to build a more structured approach without losing the flexibility that MRO procurement requires.

**Key topics:**
- Supplier rationalisation — how many is too many?
- Contract coverage vs. spot buying tradeoffs
- How to evaluate MRO suppliers beyond price
- The relationship between sourcing strategy and inventory performance
