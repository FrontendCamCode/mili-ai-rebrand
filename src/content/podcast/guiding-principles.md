After years of working with asset-intensive operations across multiple industries, certain principles emerge consistently as the difference between organisations that manage MRO well and those that don't. They're not complex. They're not proprietary. They're often overlooked.

Tim and Justin share the principles that guide their work and that they'd want every MRO practitioner to internalise.

**Key topics:**
- Data quality as the non-negotiable foundation
- Why process before technology is always the right sequence
- The importance of measuring what matters, not just what's easy
- Why outcomes — not activities — are the right focus for improvement programmes
