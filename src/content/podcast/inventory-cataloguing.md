Inventory cataloguing is the process of creating complete, accurate, standardised material records — and keeping them that way. It's foundational work that enables everything else in MRO supply chain management to function correctly.

This episode covers cataloguing as a practical discipline: the standards, the workflow, and the governance required to build and maintain a material master that teams can actually rely on.

**Key topics:**
- The anatomy of a complete material record
- Noun/modifier convention — the standard that makes search work
- Cataloguing workflow — who does what and when
- Governance — how to prevent quality from degrading after a cleanup
