Physical inventory checking — whether through periodic full stocktakes or continuous cycle counting — is the mechanism by which operations verify that their system records match physical reality. The practice is unglamorous and often underfunded. The cost of not doing it is substantial.

This episode covers the mechanics of cycle counting and stocktaking in an MRO context, with practical guidance on frequency, methodology, and what to do when discrepancies are found.

**Key topics:**
- Cycle counting vs. annual stocktake — when to use each
- ABC classification as a driver of counting frequency
- How to investigate and resolve discrepancies properly
- The connection between counting discipline and planning accuracy
