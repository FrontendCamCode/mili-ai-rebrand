The Tim & Justin Show

Tim & Justin: Gentlemen, Assemble

18 minutes

The first episode of the Lexin Solutions podcast. Tim and Justin introduce themselves, explain why they started the show, and lay out what they’re trying to accomplish: practical, no-nonsense conversations about the work of managing indirect material supply chains in asset-intensive industries.

No vendor pitches. No industry buzzwords for their own sake. Just the practical realities of making MRO work better.

In this episode:

  • Who Tim and Justin are and what they’ve spent their careers doing
  • Why they started the podcast
  • What they’ve seen work (and not work) across industries
  • What topics they plan to cover in the series ahead

Your hosts

TM

Tim McLain

Co-founder, Lexin Solutions

Supply chain specialist with extensive experience in MRO materials management, procurement strategy, and ERP implementation across mining, oil & gas, and heavy manufacturing.

JL

Co-founder, Lexin Solutions

Maintenance and EAM specialist with 15+ years connecting maintenance operations to supply chain strategy in asset-intensive industries.
