What Is MRO?

12 minutes

MRO — maintenance, repair, and operations materials — is the category of goods and services that keeps a business running without going directly into the products it makes. For asset-intensive industries, it’s one of the largest and most complex procurement categories, yet it consistently receives less attention and investment than direct spend.

This introductory episode defines MRO clearly, explains why it’s different from other procurement categories, and sets up the framework for the conversations that follow in the series.

Key topics:

  • What MRO includes (and what it doesn’t)
  • Why MRO is harder to manage than direct procurement
  • The scale of MRO spend in asset-intensive industries
  • The primary value levers — cost, availability, and working capital
