The Tim & Justin Show

Supply Chain Effectiveness KPIs

17 minutes

Most MRO operations measure cost and inventory value. Few measure effectiveness — the degree to which the supply chain is doing its job of making the right materials available at the right time. The metrics that matter most are often the ones that aren’t being tracked.

This episode covers the KPIs that give a genuine picture of supply chain performance: what they are, how to calculate them, and what to do when they reveal problems.

Key metrics covered:

  • Material availability and fill rate
  • Stockout frequency and duration
  • Emergency purchasing as a proportion of total spend
  • Free text spend percentage
  • Stocking parameter validity
  • Supplier on-time delivery vs. quoted lead time
