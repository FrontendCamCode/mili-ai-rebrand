MRO — The Good, the Bad, and the Ugly

MRO materials management is the 'too-hard basket' of most organizations. With surplus inventory valued at over $212 billion, there's significant opportunity to reduce risk and cut working capital.

Tim McLain October 22, 2024 MROinventoryworking capitaloperations

MRO materials management is the “too-hard basket” that very few people pay much attention to.

MRO Defined

The three core categories:

  • M: Maintenance Materials
  • R: Repairable Materials
  • O: Operating Materials

Industries most heavily dependent on MRO management include oil and gas, mining, chemicals, and heavy manufacturing. Businesses can achieve meaningful improvements through risk mitigation and cost reduction with proper strategies.

An Uptime Magazine statistic shows surplus inventory was valued at $136 BILLION in 2007. Projecting modest 3% annual growth, this figure reaches approximately $212 billion by 2022.

Organizations could reduce risk exposure and lower working capital expenses by at least 2.5% annually through optimized MRO management practices.
