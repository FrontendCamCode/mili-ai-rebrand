Frequently asked questions.

Contact us if you still have unanswered questions or would like to find out more about how we can help your business.

How long does it take to integrate MiLi with our ERP or CMMS?

A baseline connection, including delta extracts, can be completed within 2-4 hours using pre-built connectors and APIs. No core ERP custom code is required, and MiLi is certified by SAP.

What if we incorrectly overwrite our master data in MiLi?

All changes are staged in your dedicated MiLi instance and routed through configurable approval workflows before approved roles post them back to the system of record.

What data security and compliance standards do you meet?

MiLi is SOC 2 Type II certified, runs in ISO 27001 data centres, encrypts data in transit and at rest, supports SSO/SAML, and keeps data in-region.

When should we expect measurable ROI?

Most customers breakeven within 6 months from go live, driven by duplicate elimination and inventory optimization. Lexin has also helped clients realize ROI before go live.

How is MiLi licensed and what's included?

MiLi is sold modularly, with each solution addressing a specific business challenge. Licensing includes unlimited users, no hidden data-volume costs, support, maintenance, and new modules.

What is a MiLi Lite Pilot?

A dedicated pilot instance populated with your data so your team can test MiLi, validate the business case, and see where working capital and operational risk can be improved.
